Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register and create a pet profile? +

You can register by clicking the "Sign Up" button on the homepage. Once registered, navigate to your dashboard to add pet details such as name, type, and age.

What services does Aleef offer? +

Aleef provides user registration, pet dashboards with care tips, vaccination reminders, appointment tracking, and nearby clinic locators using HERE Maps.

Can I get reminders for vaccinations? +

Yes, we offer automatic reminders for upcoming vaccinations depend on the pet type through the calendar feature in your dashboard.

How does the nearby vet clinic locator work? +

Our locator uses HERE Maps to identify clinics near your location, displaying their names and directions.

What kind of pet care tips are available? +

Does Aleef provide a correct trusted information about the pets? +

Yes, Aleef collaborates with certified veterinarians and pet care experts to ensure all the information provided is accurate, reliable, and up-to-date. We prioritize the well-being of your pets by delivering expert-verified tips and guidelines.

Can I add multiple pets to my profile? +

Absolutely! You can manage multiple pet profiles under a single user account.

How often is the blog and news section updated? +

The blog and news section is updated weekly and sometimes daily with the latest pet-related articles, news, and expert insights.
